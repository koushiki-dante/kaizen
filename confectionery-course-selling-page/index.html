I am your teacher

Nice to meet you, I am Michelangelo di Lodovico!

For a long time people have been asking me for recipes for tasty desserts. And I deliver them, but I have come to the conclusion that this lifestyle is not my ultimate goal.

When you replicate recipes, you are equaling most of your competitors and selling ordinary desserts that you find everywhere. That is, with all the desserts looking the same, customers will buy from whoever offers the cheapest desert.

But when you master the "science" of the perfect dessert, you have the ability to create your own recipes whenever you want or need them. Unique recipes that no one else has and that will allow you to achieve your goals with more confidence than ever before.

And that's when your customers will value you the most and pay your price. Even if your competitors are seling their deserts at half the price.
